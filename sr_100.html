Building Your Own Characters

This collection of 100+ characters demonstrates the incredible diversity possible in Shadowrun. Each character shows how the core archetypes can be customized with unique backgrounds, specializations, and personal motivations. Use these examples as inspiration for creating your own runners, or drop them directly into your campaigns as NPCs, contacts, rivals, or pre-generated player characters.

Key Patterns to Notice:

  • Background Drives Behavior: Each character's corporate, military, academic, or street background shapes their approach to shadowrunning
  • Specialization Within Archetypes: Street Samurai can be snipers, brawlers, or defensive specialists
  • Cross-Archetype Teams: Successful teams combine different skills and approaches
  • Personal Stakes: The best characters have reasons beyond money for their shadowrunning
  • Interconnected Stories: Characters with compatible or conflicting backgrounds create natural drama

Using This Collection:

  • Player Characters: Pick any character as a starting point for player creation
  • NPCs: Use as contacts, rivals, or supporting characters
  • Team Templates: The complete teams provide ready-made opposition or allied groups
  • Campaign Seeds: Each character's background suggests potential storylines
  • Inspiration: Mix and match elements to create entirely new characters

Remember: The best Shadowrun characters are shaped by the world's unique blend of cyberpunk technology and fantasy magic, corporate power and street-level desperation. Let these contradictions create interesting internal conflicts and external challenges for every character you create.

Every runner has a story. Every story changes the world. What legend will you create in the shadows?
