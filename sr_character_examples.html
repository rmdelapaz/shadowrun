Building Your Own Characters

These examples showcase how the mechanical elements from our tutorial series combine with rich backstories and clear motivations to create compelling characters. Notice how each character:

  • Has clear personal stakes: Each character has reasons beyond money for their shadowrunning
  • Represents their archetype: While fitting the mechanical role, each has unique personality and approach
  • Connects to the setting: Their backgrounds tie into Shadowrun's corporate world and social issues
  • Has room to grow: Each character has advancement goals and relationship development opportunities
  • Fits team dynamics: Their skills complement each other while their personalities create interesting interactions

Character Creation Tips

  • Start with motivation: Why does this person risk their life for money?
  • Connect to the world: How has the Sixth World shaped their life experiences?
  • Balance competence and vulnerability: Great characters are good at some things and struggle with others
  • Plan for growth: Where do you want this character to be in 20 sessions?
  • Create relationships: Contacts aren't just resources - they're people who matter to your character

Remember: The best Shadowrun characters are shaped by the unique combination of high technology and ancient magic, corporate power and street-level desperation, that defines the Sixth World. Let these contradictions create interesting internal conflicts and external challenges for your characters.

Every legend starts with a choice to step into the shadows. What story will your character write?
